I breathe in inspiration from figure painting masters such as Rembrandt, Lucian Freud, and Gerhard Richter, however I infuse modern methods such as collage, photo-transfer, pastiche, multi planes, and blurring the line between drawing and painting to converge traditionalism with an element of unexpectedness. I use photos as source materials because of their indexical, documentary quality, but in a culture saturated with instant electronic images, the sub process of painting becomes especially important because it opens up a richer layer of beauty and space for interpretation.
